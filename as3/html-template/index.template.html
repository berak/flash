webcam gesture/shape recognition using hausdorff metrics.

play around with the binary threshold,
using Down or Up keys, until you get a nice filled shape.

then adjust the edge threshold (Left/Right keys), until the inner points have vanished
the less points left here, the faster we go later..

now, to record a gesture, press Enter,
clear the last record with Backspace, or all of them with Del.

there you go !! (.. hard to remember, what your hands did only seconds ago :)

sometimes it's fun to binarize not the current image, but the
diff to a previous frame, pressing Space will capture such a 'background' image,
another Space will reset to a black buffer.

if you like to play around whith it,
a javacrypt function called 'gestureFound' will get called each frame.

please mail me, if you've done something funny || useful.
